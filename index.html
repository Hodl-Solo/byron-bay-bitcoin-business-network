About the Network

The Byron Bay Bitcoin Business Network is a grassroots initiative to connect Bitcoiners with local businesses that accept Bitcoin. Whether you're a local, a tourist, or a new resident, this is your guide to spending sats in one of Australia's most iconic beach towns.

We're inspired by the global Bitcoin-only movement — no stablecoins, no shitcoins, just Bitcoin. If your business accepts Bitcoin and you'd like to be listed, reach out below.
